RISK ON RADAR - CUSTOMER CONTRACT PACK
Last updated: 14 July 2026

BEFORE SHARING CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
[ ] Mutual NDA - only when confidential information will be shared before the main agreement is signed.

FOR A PAID PILOT
[ ] Pilot Agreement / Pilot Order Form - scope, dates, users, price, VAT, success criteria, review responsibility, confidentiality, liability, termination, and conversion decision.
[ ] Data Processing Agreement (DPA) - attach when Risk on Radar processes personal data on the customer's behalf.
[ ] Security Schedule - factual description of hosting, access, encryption, isolation, backups, incidents, retention, deletion, LLM use, and subprocessors.
[ ] Subprocessor List - current customer-data vendors, purpose, processing location, and change-notification method.
[ ] AI Intended-Use and Limitations Statement - evidence research copilot, engineer review required, no certification, no guaranteed completeness, no autonomous safety decision.
[ ] Data Schedule - customer inputs, prohibited data, ownership, permitted processing, export, return, and deletion.

FOR A SUBSCRIPTION AFTER THE PILOT
[ ] Master Subscription Agreement (MSA) / B2B SaaS Terms.
[ ] Subscription Order Form - plan, seats/workspace, price, term, renewal, support, and billing.
[ ] Data Processing Agreement (DPA), if applicable.
[ ] Security Schedule.
[ ] Subprocessor List.
[ ] Service Level Agreement (SLA) - only when uptime/support commitments are sold.
[ ] Acceptable Use Policy - may be incorporated by reference in the MSA.
[ ] AI Intended-Use and Limitations Statement - may be incorporated in the MSA/product documentation.

PUBLIC WEBSITE DOCUMENTS - NOT A SUBSTITUTE FOR CUSTOMER CONTRACTS
[ ] Legal Notice / Company Information.
[ ] Privacy Notice.
[ ] Cookie Notice and consent controls, where needed.
[ ] Public Terms of Service for self-serve accounts.
[ ] Public Subprocessor Page.

INTERNAL SIGN-OFF BEFORE SENDING
[ ] Correct Risk on Radar legal entity, address, KVK number, VAT ID, and signatory.
[ ] Commercial terms match the approved quote.
[ ] Liability cap matches the insurance position and has legal review.
[ ] No unsupported standards, certification, safety, accuracy, or compliance claim.
[ ] Customer confidential data is not used for model training unless expressly agreed.
[ ] Signed copy stored in the restricted customer contract folder with renewal/notice dates recorded.
