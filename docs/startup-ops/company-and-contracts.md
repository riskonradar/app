# Company, Ownership, And Contract Suite

## Incorporation Instruction List

Ask the notary and tax adviser to cover these points in one coordinated instruction:

- Whether each founder should use a personal holding B.V.
- Operating-company name, registered office, purpose, share classes, and nominal value.
- Founder share allocation and any pre-incorporation contribution.
- Director appointment and whether each director can bind the company alone or jointly.
- UBO registration and evidence.
- Financial year and initial extended financial year, if useful.
- DGA employment/payroll and a documented customary-salary position.
- Whether a fiscal unity is useful later, not automatically at formation.
- Founder vesting mechanics that work under Dutch company and tax law.
- Future option pool mechanics and whether a STAK is appropriate only when actually needed.

Do not sign customer or supplier obligations as `B.V. i.o.` without understanding that the
individual may remain liable until the company adopts the contract.

## Founders And Shareholders Agreement

The agreement should address at least:

- Roles, time commitments, milestones, and founder compensation.
- Equity, reverse vesting, cliff, credit for past service, and acceleration.
- Good-leaver and bad-leaver events and objective share valuation mechanics.
- IP assignment, further assurances, confidentiality, and conflicts with old employers.
- Board composition, information rights, quorum, reserved matters, and spending authority.
- Deadlock escalation, mediation, buyout mechanisms, and last-resort outcomes.
- New share issues, pre-emption, dilution, option pool, and future financing.
- Transfer restrictions, right of first refusal, tag-along, and drag-along.
- Incapacity, death, misconduct, non-solicit, and enforceable restrictive covenants.
- Dividend policy, founder loans, expenses, and related-party transactions.
- Governing law, disputes, amendments, and accession by new shareholders.

Avoid a bare 50/50 split with no deadlock mechanism. Avoid vesting language copied from a
US template without Dutch tax and notarial implementation.

## IP Chain Of Title

The operating company needs signed assignments from every founder and contractor covering:

- Existing and future source code, models, prompts, schemas, taxonomies, documentation,
  designs, research outputs, databases, and database rights.
- Copyright, neighbouring rights, design rights, inventions, know-how, trade secrets,
  domains, trade names, and rights to apply for registration.
- Further-assurance obligations and waiver/non-assertion of moral rights to the extent
  legally permitted.
- Confirmation that no previous employer, university, client, or grant owns the work.
- A schedule of excluded pre-existing materials and third-party/open-source dependencies.

Maintain an IP register with asset, author, creation date, employer/contractor agreement,
repository/location, third-party inputs, licence, and owner. The whitepaper author and all
diagram creators should assign or license the required rights to the operating company.

Run a BOIP/EUIPO clearance search before investing in the brand. KVK trade-name registration
does not create the same protection as a trademark. BOIP registrations cover Benelux for ten
years and can be renewed. See the official [trademark guide](https://business.gov.nl/regulations/register-trademark/).

## Customer Contract Architecture

Use an MSA plus short order form for negotiated B2B deals. Use equivalent click-through
B2B SaaS terms for self-serve plans. Keep product, privacy, security, and commercial terms
consistent across both routes.

### Master Subscription Agreement / SaaS Terms

- Company identity, service description, users, fees, taxes, payment, renewal, and changes.
- Licence/access rights, customer responsibilities, acceptable use, and account security.
- Clear customer-data ownership and a narrow licence to process it to provide the service.
- No model training on confidential customer data unless separately and expressly agreed.
- Confidentiality, security commitments, privacy/DPA precedence, and subprocessors.
- AI and evidence limitations: suggestions are incomplete, may be wrong, require competent
  engineering review, and are not certification, legal advice, or a safety guarantee.
- Customer remains responsible for engineering decisions, S/O/D ratings, validation,
  regulatory compliance, and use in safety-critical workflows.
- Service levels, support, maintenance, beta features, and third-party dependencies.
- IP ownership, feedback licence, infringement claims, and customer-input warranties.
- Warranties, disclaimers, indemnities, liability cap, excluded damages, and carve-outs.
- Term, suspension, termination, export, retrieval, deletion, and survival.
- Data Act switching clauses and machine-readable export categories.
- Governing law, venue, notices, assignment, force majeure, and order of precedence.

Liability wording needs **professional sign-off**. A disclaimer alone will not remove all
liability. Ensure the contractual cap and excluded activities align with insurance cover.

### Data Processing Agreement

- Controller/processor roles by data category and processing purpose.
- GDPR Article 28 instructions, confidentiality, security, assistance, audit, breach notice,
  return/deletion, and subprocessor authorisation.
- Processing annex: subjects, data, purposes, duration, retention, and special restrictions.
- Technical and organisational measures.
- Current subprocessor list and change-notification mechanism.
- International-transfer mechanism, SCC modules, transfer impact assessment, and safeguards.

Risk on Radar will usually be controller for accounts, billing, prospects, product analytics,
and its own security logs. It may be processor for customer users and uploaded FMEA/asset
data processed solely under customer instructions. Document each role rather than calling
the company only a processor.

### Pilot Order Form

- One bounded asset/component and one defined workflow.
- Start/end date, named customer reviewer, and permitted users.
- Inputs the customer will provide and prohibited data.
- Deliverables, success metrics, meeting cadence, and conversion decision.
- Fee, expenses, credit toward subscription, and tax treatment.
- Data deletion/return and publication/reference rights.
- No production safety reliance during a design-partner pilot unless explicitly insured
  and contracted after validation.

### Other Templates

- Mutual NDA with a residuals clause only if counsel recommends it.
- Contractor agreement with deliverables, genuine independence, IP, confidentiality,
  security, data protection, and warranty of original work.
- Employment agreement and handbook before the first employee.
- Security schedule/questionnaire response library.
- AI system factsheet and customer-facing intended-use/limitations statement.
- Source takedown and rights-holder contact procedure.

## Employment And Contractors

When hiring the first employee, register as an employer with the Tax Administration and
notify KVK, set up payroll, check the applicable CAO, provide written employment terms,
complete health-and-safety obligations, and arrange sickness/absence support. Use the
[official first-employee checklist](https://business.gov.nl/staff/employing-staff/employing-your-first-staff/).

Do not treat a founder-like full-time worker under company direction as a freelancer merely
to avoid payroll. Dutch enforcement of false self-employment is active and fines may apply
from 2026. Assess the real working relationship and preserve the assessment using the
[official guidance](https://business.gov.nl/starting-your-business/starting-as-a-self-employed-professional/avoid-false-self-employment/).

## Insurance Order

1. Professional indemnity: core cover for erroneous software/advice causing financial loss.
2. Cyber/data liability: breach response, business interruption, privacy claims, and vendors.
3. AVB/business liability: injury or property damage, especially for customer-site work.
4. D&O: add when external investors/directors or material contracts justify it.
5. Employer policies and sickness/absence cover before employees.

Disclose the real use of LLMs, aviation evidence, and engineering decision support to the
broker. A policy that excludes safety-critical software, professional advice, AI, or North
American claims may provide little practical protection. The official distinction between
AVB and professional indemnity is summarised by
[Business.gov.nl](https://business.gov.nl/running-your-business/insurance/business-liability-insurance/).
