# Fundraising And Bootstrap Notes

The question is not "investors or no investors." The question is what money unlocks that you cannot unlock by staying lean.

## What Money Could Unlock

### Founder Time

If both founders are part-time, this is the biggest unlock. Funding can buy focused execution time, which may matter more than tools.

### Commercial Data Access

Publisher TDM access, commercial literature licensing, or specialized engineering databases may become necessary if the product depends on full-text evidence at scale. Treat this as a quoted vendor cost, not a guessed line item.

### Customer Acquisition

Funding can support industry events, travel, outbound tooling, sales material, and possibly a fractional sales advisor. Do not hire full-time sales until the founders can sell the product themselves.

### Product Credibility

Security, observability, legal docs, privacy posture, and a more polished demo can matter for B2B engineering buyers.

### Specialist Contractors

Use contractors before employees:
- Brand designer
- Frontend/product designer
- Data engineer
- Security reviewer
- Reliability engineering specialist
- Technical writer for investor/customer material

## When To Bootstrap

Bootstrap if:
- You can keep burn below EUR 250-500/month.
- You can reach customers directly.
- The product can be validated without expensive licensed data.
- You do not yet know the buyer or pricing.
- Investor prep would distract from customer learning.

## When To Raise

Raise if:
- Customers clearly want the product but data licensing blocks delivery.
- Founder time is the limiting factor.
- You have strong pilot demand and need to move fast.
- You need credibility/security/legal work to pass enterprise evaluation.
- You can explain exactly how EUR 100k, EUR 250k, or EUR 500k changes the company.

## What Not To Raise For

- A nicer logo.
- Generic marketing.
- Premature sales hires.
- Infra that can run cheaply.
- Team tools that free plans already cover.
- Avoiding founder-led sales.

## Brand Recommendation

Use a simple credible wordmark now. Avoid a full agency engagement until positioning is validated.

Recommended path:
1. Use a clean temporary brand system internally.
2. Spend EUR 0-300 only if the current logo is actively hurting credibility.
3. After customer segment and narrative are clearer, hire a good independent designer for EUR 1k-5k.
4. Avoid agency-level spend until after funding or meaningful revenue.
